Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

This site collects only what it needs to do its job: respond to inquiries, send the lead-magnet booklet, and track basic, anonymous usage so the site can be improved over time.

What we collect

  • Form submissions: when you submit the lead-magnet form (“The 7 Signs Your Business Is Disconnected”), we collect your email address and, optionally, your first name and biggest current challenge.
  • Strategy Call bookings: when you book a Strategy Call, scheduling and contact information passes through our scheduling tool (Calendly). See Calendly’s privacy policy for details.
  • Anonymous analytics: page views, referrer, country, and device type, used in aggregate to understand which content is useful.

How we use it

  • To deliver the booklet you requested.
  • To respond to questions you send us.
  • To send occasional emails about systems, operations, and the work — only if you opted in by submitting a form. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.
  • To improve the site based on aggregate, anonymous usage patterns.

Who we share with

We use a small number of trusted services to operate the site:

  • Hostinger — web hosting
  • Calendly — Strategy Call scheduling
  • GoHighLevel (GPF Business Systems) — email delivery and CRM for lead-magnet recipients
  • Google Fonts — typography (no personal data shared)

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for advertising.

Your rights

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in any email.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.

Email g@gillianparkinsonf.com for any of these.

Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies — only what’s required for the site to function and for anonymous, aggregate analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change. Material changes will be communicated to active subscribers by email.

Contact

Questions about privacy: g@gillianparkinsonf.com
